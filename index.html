ABOUT US

Global Recyclers Inc. is a second-generation plastic trader and recycler in the United States. We are a leading supplier of high-quality plastic raw materials from North America to diverse industries worldwide. Our company was established to provide excellent services and solutions to the plastic recycling industry.

We specialize in procuring, processing, and supplying various types of plastics, including PET, ABS, HDPE, LDPE, PVC, and PP. Our experienced team of professionals is dedicated to ensuring that our customers receive the best possible products and services.

Our company has a strong focus on customer satisfaction. We work closely with our customers to understand their needs and requirements. Our team of experts is committed to ensuring personalized support and services, including logistics and inventory management.

We give the utmost importance to preserving the environment. Therefore, we play our part in reducing global warming by actively participating in recycling different plastic materials and reducing landfill waste. We can make a dedicated attempt to heal our planet through recycling.
